## Project Goal

Prototype a push-assist system that reduces manual effort while keeping motion
smooth, safe, and predictable.

## What I Built

- Mechanical frame with assist mechanism integration
- Initial motor and controller setup for variable assist
- Basic control logic for throttle and response smoothing
- Early physical safety checks around startup and stopping behavior

## Results

The prototype demonstrates consistent assist response during short test runs
and improved handling compared to fully manual pushing.

## Next Iteration

- Improve torque tuning for heavier loads
- Add better battery and thermal monitoring
- Refine enclosure and cable routing for reliability
